Aims

This is an introduction to Animation.

By the end of this unit you will learn:

  • Level 3
    • (H&P)Knows the difference between Hardware and Software
    • (IT)Uses IT independantly to organise digital content.
    • (IT)Shows an awareness of Quality of Digital content.
    • (IT)Uses a variety of software to create digital content.
    • (IT)Makes improvements based on feedback.
  • Level 4
    • (H&P)Understands why computers are used for Word processing
    • (IT)Makes judgements about digital content when evaluationing it and repurposing it for a given audience.
    • (IT)Understands the potential of IT for collaboration.
    • (IT)Uses criteria to evaluate the quality of solutions.
    • (IT)Can identify improvements, independently aking some refinements to the solution.
  • Level 5
    • (IT)Evaluates the appropriateness of software to achieving a goal.
    • (IT)Recognises the ethical issues surrounding word processing. Intellectual property and referencing.
    • (IT)Designs criteria to evaluate the quality of solutions.
  • Level 6
    • (IT)Justifies the appropriateness of software to achieving a given goal.
    • (IT)Evaluates the trustworthiness of digital source material.
    • (IT)Considers the effects of visual design features on the usability of produced digital materials.
    • (IT)Designs criteria for another user to evaluate the quality of solutions.
